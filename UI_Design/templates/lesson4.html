Cash Flow Types

Understanding Cash Flow from Financing

Cash flow from financing activities reflects the inflows and outflows of cash resulting from activities such as issuing or repurchasing stocks, issuing or repaying debt/loans, and paying dividends. It indicates how a company raises capital and how it distributes it to shareholders and creditors.
