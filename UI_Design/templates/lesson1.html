What is a Cash Flow Statement?

A cash flow statement is a financial statement that provides data regarding all cash inflows a company receives from its operations and investment sources, as well as all cash outflows that pay for business activities and investments during a given period.

There are three main parts to a cash flow statement: cash flow from operating activities, cash flow from investing activities, and cash flow from financing activities.
